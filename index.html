Tessellate

A fun game for your spatial short-term memory.

This game has 15 levels. At each level a pattern of tiles will appear on the screen for a moment. Remember the correct tiles appeared in the matrix and then repeat the pattern by tapping on the correct tiles. You will advance to the next level(more tiles to remember) only if you correctly tap all the tiles of the pattern of that level. If you are unable to pass a level you will drop down to the previous level. The game offers a reload and a hint button. The reload button(can be used only at the beginning of a level) reloads the current level. The hint button reveals the remainning tiles of the level for a moment. Your game ends when you have used all(4) your hints.

+ 1000xlevel for passing a level
- 500 for failing a level
- 200 for reloading a level

Hints can be used to escape the -500 penalty with no loss of points. Your game ends when you have used all(4) your hints. Try to remember the correct pattern and score as much as possible. Random tile color on every new game along with random backgrounds on every level require concentration, focus and mental awareness.
